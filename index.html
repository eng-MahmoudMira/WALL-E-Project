WALL-E: Landing On Mars

Project Overview

A concept monitoring system for autonomous waste collection and recycling on Mars. The hub coordinates scouting robots, collects waste streams, and tracks recycling performance across key materials like fabrics, metals, plastics, packaging foams, EVA, and structural elements.

About the Hub

The hub orchestrates collection routes, staging, sorting, and conversion to usable products for habitat construction and mission logistics. This static prototype focuses on UI and information architecture.
